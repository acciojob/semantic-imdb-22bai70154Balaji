Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets through dream-sharing technology. He is offered a chance to erase his criminal past by performing an impossible task called "inception"— planting an idea into someone's subconscious instead of stealing one.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio stars as Dom Cobb, supported by Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page (Elliot Page), Tom Hardy, Ken Watanabe, Cillian Murphy, and Marion Cotillard.
